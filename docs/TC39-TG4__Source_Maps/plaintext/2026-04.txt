[21:19:12.0990] <Simon Zünd>
Class scopes don't really make sense: 1) You can't pause in them, unless you count initializer expressions, 2) they don't show up in stack traces, again, except initializer expressions. Classes are inherently tied to types, which is an explicit non-goal of the "scopes" proposal.

[21:19:48.0655] <Simon Zünd>
e.g. pausing in DevTools/Firefox inside a method will not show you a "class scope" in the scopes view. You'll see that `this` is of type `class C`, but thats probably it.
