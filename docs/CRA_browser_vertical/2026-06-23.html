Hey folks, for Annex K I've started collating the cryptographic mechanisms mentioned in the Annex K draft doc, ACM v2, and Andrew Whalley (Google)'s Chromium spreadsheet linked previously. You can see my work in progress in this sheet: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1da5asGBYjlPGPagD6R8hrYOpqGFBpd6TvxkeD0GcTu0/edit?gid=0#gid=0

This is ~1/3rd complete (e.g., the items from the draft are all here, but only a small fraction of the ACM or Chromium sheet are covered yet). I have very little cryptography domain knowledge, so I expect the first pass of this will result in several duplicates, entries we won't ultimately want/need (e.g., stuff that no browser has actually implemented), and things that a just a little bit wrong. I imagine a second pass will filter it down and fill in the gaps on things we want to keep (e.g., I had trouble tracking down a couple of specs).

Note that I'm not expressing any opinion what ought to make into Annex K, but I thought it would be helpful to have a comprehensive list of candidates to work from. Comments open are open on that link. Thank you!
