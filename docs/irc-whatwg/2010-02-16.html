02:37<Hixie>I send a FIN (shutdown with SHUT_WR), then I wait until the socket is ready to read and recv() returns zero, then I call close().
02:37<Hixie>but what does the other side do?

02:49<Hixie>hm, it seems i'm wrong about close() clearing the _send_ buffer

02:49<Hixie>so
02:49<Hixie>a simple graceful shutdown can be done just by one side or the other sending a FIN
02:50<Hixie>then it waiting for recv()=0 and calling close()
02:50<Hixie>and the other side just calling close()
