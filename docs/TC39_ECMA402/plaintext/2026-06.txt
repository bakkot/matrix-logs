A few updates from the in-person WHATWG WHATNOT meeting that may be of interest to TG2:
- [Spell check custom dictionary](https://github.com/whatwg/html/pull/12590) advanced to Stage 2
- [Localized time formatting without JavaScript](https://github.com/whatwg/html/issues/12591) was approved for Stage 1. This is proposing to add attributes corresponding to the Unicode MessageFormat datetime formatting options to the HTML `<time>` element, such that the rendered contents of the element would become a localized representation of the element's `datetime` attribute value.
- [DOM Localization](https://github.com/whatwg/html/issues/12584) was approved for Stage 1.
