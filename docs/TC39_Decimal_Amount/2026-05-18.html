FYI, I added the following slide to the Amount presentation to address waldemar's review comments:

Open issue: Converting 5 grams to tonnes results in 0.0000049999999999999996 tonnes

As pointed out by Waldemar Horvat, using Number multiplication for conversions can lead to surprising results:

5 * 1e-6 === 0.0000049999999999999996

We are open to iterating on and improving the ConvertUnitValue algorithm during Stage 2, as long as we don't end up imposing an implicit requirement on implementations to support arbitrary precision arithmetic, or support for rational values.
